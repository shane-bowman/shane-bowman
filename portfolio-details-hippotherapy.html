Hippotherapy Flutter App

Description: Collaborated with a team of five peers (Seth Palmer, Gia Diep, Jaxon Prouse, Denali Therrien, Aron Szabo) over two semesters to develop a Flutter-based mobile application for Hippotherapy assessments, streamlining data entry and patient progress tracking for client Mylena Medeiros at Saskatchewan Polytechnic. I was responsible for designing and implementing the login interface, Firebase authentication, patient archive system, and in-app tutorial, ensuring secure access and user onboarding. Conducted client meetings to prioritize features like session evaluations and data export, employing test-driven development (TDD) with Flutter, ASP.NET, Firebase, and AWS deployment.

Reflection: This project significantly enhanced my Flutter development and teamwork skills. Leading the authentication and tutorial features taught me to balance security with user experience, while TDD ensured robust functionality. Coordinating with the client and team to define priorities deepened my ability to align technical solutions with user needs. I plan to leverage TDD and Firebase in future projects to enhance scalability and real-time features.

Skills Demonstrated: Flutter, Firebase, ASP.NET, test-driven development, authentication, UI/UX design, teamwork, client collaboration.
